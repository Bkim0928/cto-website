InSite Live: Follow these steps to connect to your broadcast

1. If you are in a GSA office, connect your computer to a docking station. Don't watch the video on a wireless connection ("wifi") if you are in a GSA office.

2. Click the link below based on where you are viewing the video from.

  • I am viewing from a field office
  • I am viewing on VPN
  • I am viewing from a Regional Office Building
  • I am watching from an office in Washington, D.C.
  • I am watching from a Horizon desktop (VDI)

3. If you are prompted to save, select OK.

4. Click the saved file at the bottom of your browser window. The broadcast should start in VLC media player.
