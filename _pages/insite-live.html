InSite Live: Follow these steps to connect to your broadcast

1. Address these special situations first:

  • If you are in a GSA office, connect your computer to a docking station while watching the video, instead of using wireless ("wifi").
  • If you are using a Mac, connect to a Horizon desktop (VDI), and then follow the instructions for Horizon in step 2. (If you need information about using Horizon on Mac, read this InSite page: Horizon VDI on a Mac)

2. Click the link below based on where you are watching the video:

  • I am watching from a field office
  • I am teleworking and watching on VPN
  • I am watching from a Regional Office Building
  • I am watching from an office in Washington, D.C.
  • I am watching from a Horizon desktop (VDI)

3. If you are prompted to save, select OK.

4. Click the saved file at the bottom of your browser window. VLC media player should load on your computer.

5. If a dialog with the title "Privacy and Network Access Policy" is displayed, uncheck both boxes and click "Continue".

6. If a dialog with the title "VLC Media Player Updates" is displayed, click "No".
